Why create VibeCheck?

My friend, Arman Rafati, and me, Shayan sarnevesht had an idea to help connect people in spite of the pandemic online by way of mutual interests and hobbies between people. With this project, users can enter in their hobbies, interests, age and other data points about them that our algorithm uses to pair people. This way, people can become friends online, and will be able to hang out when the pandemic ends and it's safe to meet people in person. We hope you enjoy.
